Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

Amulet is built so that your cards stay yours.

What we store, and where

Everything you create in Amulet — spreads, questions, notes, photos, your card of the day, your practice settings — is stored locally on your device and, if you use iCloud, synced through your own private iCloud account. We do not operate servers and cannot read, access, or recover your content.

The AI reads on-device too

Thread readings are generated by Apple Intelligence directly on your device. Nothing you write, ask, or pull is sent anywhere to be processed.

What we collect

  • Anonymous usage analytics (TelemetryDeck): anonymized signals such as “spread saved” or “onboarding completed”, used to understand which features matter. TelemetryDeck does not use device identifiers and cannot identify you. Never the content you write. See telemetrydeck.com/privacy.
  • Purchases: your one-time purchase is processed entirely by Apple. We never see your payment details.

Photos and camera

Photos you attach to a spread are saved into your journal only — on your device and in your private iCloud. Camera access is used solely to take that photo, at your request.

Notifications

Daily reminders are scheduled locally on your device. There are no push servers.

What we don’t do

  • No accounts. No email collection. No ads. No tracking across apps.
  • We never sell data. There is no data to sell.

Your rights

Delete the app and your local data is gone. Manage or delete iCloud data in Settings → Apple ID → iCloud. For anything else, write to esthersarasua.dev@gmail.com.

Changes

If our practices change, we will update this page and the “Last updated” date above.
