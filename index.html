01 / The deck

Deck in hand,
or not.

Draw your daily card inside the app —real shuffle, reversals included— or log the reading you just did with your physical deck. Either way, Amulet keeps it and helps you read it.

Two ways to draw, one journal.

  • Daily cardA real shuffle inside the app, upright or reversed, with its symbol and reading.
  • Your physical readingFreely log the spread you did with your own deck, with its question and date.
  • All 78 arcanaThe whole deck on hand: 22 major and 56 minor, to check and learn.
02 / Patterns

What repeats
says more than one reading.

One card is a moment. Many readings, kept month after month, are a pattern. Amulet counts what returns —cards, suits, reversals— and shows what you'd miss on your own: "The Tower has come up 4 times this month."

  • Cards that insist: which arcana shows up again and again, and when it started
  • Suits and reversals: if your readings lean into one suit, or something keeps landing reversed
  • The month's theme: where your month went, by your own cards
03 / Meanings

Every arcana,
from the inside.

Meanings for all 78 arcana, upright and reversed, written to make you reflect —not to scare you. No closed jargon: clear text that helps you understand what's in front of you.

04 / Privacy

What you write
stays with you.

Your journal is yours. Amulet asks for no account and sends your readings to no server. What you note lives on your phone, and you decide whether to back it up.

No account

No sign-ups, no passwords. Open the app and you're in.

Private iCloud

Your journal backs up to your own iCloud, encrypted and in your name.

All on device

Meanings and readings work on your phone, offline.

One purchase

Yours forever. No subscription, no ads.
